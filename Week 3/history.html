The history of bears

Bears are mammals of the family Ursidae. They are found in North America, South America, Europe and Asia. The polar bear is the most carnivouros while the panda feeds on a diet of bamboo. They are usually solitary animals. They possess an excellent sense of smell and can run, climb and swim well considering their build.
